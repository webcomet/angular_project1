FESTV | FAQ

Ticket Types

Q: Whats the difference between FESTNATION and FESTV? A: FESTNATION is our pay-as-you-go all access global festival hub: where you can find out about hundred's of festivals globally, browse their programs, see whats playing, engage, support and interact with a global film festival community that loves indie film. FESTV is our subscription video on-demand service where you get to stream collections from Festivals for $3.99 a month.

Q: What the difference between a VIP and an ALL ACCESS ticket? A: VIP tickets are for festival invited attendees only. ALL ACCESS tickets are open to the general public.

Q: When I buy a VIP ticket I can only watch a movie once? A: It’s part of the many measures we go through to protect the filmmakers’ distribution rights. We are offering the privilege to screen festival movies that are still seeking distribution and need to make sure those movies can attain that goal. If you’d like to see the movie again hang around… I bet you will find it in our ALL ACCESS festival catalogs very soon.

Q: If I have a VIP ticket and I fall asleep during the movie can I watch it again? A: Yes. Once you push play, you have 48hrs to watch before you lose access.

Q: When I am on a filmmaker’s movie page there is a contact filmmaker button. What is this for? A: This is a funding platform designed for filmmakers’ to shop their next projects. If you liked the current movie and are interested in helping this filmmaker make their next, then click on the link and we will put you two in touch.

Q: I see a FESTV subscription based (SVOD) service coming soon? A: Yes it is! FESTV will offer unlimited access to entire Festival's collections on a monthly/recurring basis for $3.99/Month or $42/Yr.

Devices Supported

Q: Do I need to be connected to the Internet or Wi-Fi to stream videos from FESTV? A: Yes, you will need a broadband connection (LAN, DSL or cable modem) to watch content on Windows or Mac OS. For mobile devices, you will need a Wi-Fi connection or a 3G connection or higher.

Q: What browsers are supported by FESTV? A: Windows and Mac OS users, we recommend using the latest versions of Firefox, Safari and Google Chrome or Internet Explorer 9 and higher.

Q: Which mobile devices are supported by FESTV? A: All of FESTV is mobile friendly. Videos will play back in a web browser on supported iOS and Android devices.

Q: Can I simultaneously sign into multiple devices and stream content from FESTV? A: Yes, you can with the same username login.

Q: How do I play movies on my TV? A: We recommend buying an affordable Apple TV device then you can stream your movie from your iPad, iPhone, or Mac computer to your TV through the function Air Play. Or if you’re a Droid user you can accomplish the same thing with a Roku device or Google Chromecast connected to your TV. If all else fails, you can always connect with an HDMI wire from your computer.

Q: When I stream a video from FESTV, the video player suddenly stops working. What should I do? A: If you are experiencing technical difficulties with our video player, please make sure you have the correct system requirements. Also, please try closing your web browser then reopening it and signing in again. If the problem persists, please contact us at support@festv.us

Technical Specs

Q: Does FESTV edit videos for content? A: No, we do not.

Q: Does your media player provide subtitles? A: We do not offer subtitles yet, we will soon!

Q: Does your media player provide closed captioning? A: Yes, we offer close captioning. Click the "CC" icon in the media player to see if it's available for your selection.

Q: Do you offer HD downloads of the films to purchase? A: No, not at this time.

Q: Why isn’t there a rating on the movie, like PG-13? A: The reason is most indie films cannot afford to pay for a rating from the MPAA. If you want to watch a movie with your children, we recommend you viewing it first or selecting the movie from a children focused festival.

Billing

Q: How does FESTV billing work? A: When you rent videos, you will be able to stream them anytime during the specified rental period.

Q: What types of payments do you accept? A: We accept most major credit cards.

Q: My credit card is not working? A: Your payment method may have failed. If we aren’t able to charge your payment method successfully, then your purchase won’t go through, and you’ll need to try making the purchase again. If you have questions about the status of your purchase, contact us, billing@festv.us so we can take a look on our end.

Q: Is FESTV available internationally? Can I pay using a foreign currency? A: Yes, you can enjoy streaming our festivals’ catalogs from anywhere globally. Additional fees, including foreign transaction fees, may be charged by your bank.

Q: I purchased a ticket and didn't have the time to watch anything. Do you offer refunds? A: Not at this time.

Q: How do Promo Codes work? A: If you’ve received a promo code, you can redeem it directly in the purchase window, enter the code, and click the apply button. The purchase window will then update to reflect the price of $00.00 before you finalize the purchase.

My Account
