How it Works: it's easy.

Be part of an exclusive network of tuned in fest-goers, patrons, high pass ticket holders, industry insiders, and vips who love and breathe Festival culture.

Attendees get access to more than just FILMS; they get direct access to the filmmakers who made them, and their next projects.

Festivals who programmed your film (this year or years past) invite you to come back and remain a part of their Festival.

Upload your metadata and film in 1 easy step on 1 simple page.

The Festival then bundles your content to stream.

Attendees discover and share your film.

What You Get

  • Exposure
  • Paid
  • To watch your Peers Films
  • Your film and name linked to the great festivals you've played at
  • To connect with affluent patrons and industry insiders about your next project
  • To play your film anywhere; our terms are non-exclusive
  • To join a community devoted to film festivals and indie film globally

How You Get Paid

Filmmakers get 30% of every ticket sold.

Revenue is apportioned amongst every filmmaker whose film is included in the ticket, as determined per stream/view.

Filmmakers are granted Complimentary Access to select VIP tickets as a bonus for participating.

If you have uploaded your film to a festival and have not registered, please do so. You must Sign Up to get paid.

Indie Filmmakers miss out on having their movies viewed by the people they want to see it most.
